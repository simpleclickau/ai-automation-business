## Research Notes

### Inspra.ai Analysis
- **Business Model:** AI voice agents for customer service and sales
- **Key Features:** No-code platform, CRM integrations, real-time analytics
- **Pricing:** Usage-based model
- **Target Market:** SMBs to enterprise
- **Differentiators:** Ease of use, voice quality, integration ecosystem

### Competitor Landscape
- **Retell AI:** Developer-focused, API-first, $0.10/min
- **Synthflow AI:** No-code, $29-1,250/month, strong visual builder
- **Bland AI:** Usage-based, $0.09/min, developer-friendly
- **Voiceflow:** Conversational design, $50-625/month, visual workflow
- **Vapi:** Real-time voice API, $0.05/min base + LLM costs

### Technology Stack Insights
- **STT:** AssemblyAI offers best accuracy/cost balance
- **LLM:** GPT-4o mini and Gemini 2.0 Flash provide optimal performance/cost
- **TTS:** ElevenLabs for premium quality, Cartesia for low-latency
- **Orchestration:** Vapi provides comprehensive platform capabilities
- **Cost Structure:** 56-78% gross margins achievable
