### 3. Technology Stack Research

**Location:** `/research/technology-stack.md`

**Recommended Stack:**
- **STT:** AssemblyAI (best accuracy/cost balance)
- **LLM:** GPT-4o mini / Gemini 2.0 Flash (optimal performance/cost)
- **TTS:** ElevenLabs (premium quality) / Cartesia (low-latency)
- **Orchestration:** Vapi (comprehensive platform)

**Economics:**
- Cost per 5-min call: ~$0.22
- Achievable gross margins: 56-78% at $0.50-1.00/call pricing
- LLM-agnostic architecture for flexibility
