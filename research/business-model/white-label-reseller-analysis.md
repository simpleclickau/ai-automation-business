# White-Label Reseller Business Model Analysis

**Research Date:** November 2024  
**Critical Discovery:** Inspra.ai and many Australian AI voice agent companies are **white-labeling Retell AI**  
**Purpose:** Determine optimal business model for Articulate AI (build proprietary vs. resell)

---

## Executive Summary

**KEY FINDING:** Inspra.ai is a **Retell AI reseller**, not a proprietary platform. They charge $0.18-0.25 AUD per call while Retell AI's actual cost for a 5-minute call is $0.55 AUD. This means:

1. **Inspra loses money on per-call charges** (or has enterprise volume discounts)
2. **The $499/month base fee is their actual revenue source**
3. **Most Australian "AI voice agent platforms" are resellers**, not builders

**Strategic Implications for Articulate AI:**
- **Option A:** Build proprietary (like LiveKit + custom stack) - higher margins, more control
- **Option B:** White-label Retell AI - faster to market, lower development cost
- **Option C:** Hybrid - start with Retell, build proprietary over time

---

## Inspra.ai Business Model Breakdown

### Pricing Structure

**Monthly Plan:**
- Setup fee: $1,999 AUD (one-time)
- Monthly base: $499 AUD
- Per-call: $0.25 AUD

**Yearly Plan:**
- Setup fee: $1,999 AUD (one-time)
- Monthly base: $349 AUD (30% discount)
- Per-call: $0.18 AUD

### Retell AI Underlying Cost (AUD)

**Per-Minute Pricing:**
- Base rate: $0.07 USD/min = **$0.11 AUD/min**
- 5-minute call: $0.55 AUD
- 10-minute call: $1.10 AUD

**Volume Discounts (Retell AI):**
- $0-3,000/month: $0.07 USD/min
- $3,000+/month: Custom pricing (likely $0.05-0.06 USD/min)

### Cost Analysis: 100 Calls/Month (5 min average = 500 minutes)

**Inspra Revenue:**
- Monthly plan: $499 + (100 × $0.25) = $524 AUD
- Yearly plan: $349 + (100 × $0.18) = $367 AUD

**Retell AI Cost:**
- 500 minutes × $0.11 AUD/min = **$55 AUD**

**Inspra Gross Profit:**
- Monthly plan: $524 - $55 = **$469 AUD (89% margin)**
- Yearly plan: $367 - $55 = **$312 AUD (85% margin)**

### Cost Analysis: 1,000 Calls/Month (5,000 minutes)

**Inspra Revenue:**
- Monthly plan: $499 + (1,000 × $0.25) = $749 AUD
- Yearly plan: $349 + (1,000 × $0.18) = $529 AUD

**Retell AI Cost (with volume discount):**
- 5,000 minutes × $0.08 AUD/min (estimated volume discount) = **$400 AUD**

**Inspra Gross Profit:**
- Monthly plan: $749 - $400 = **$349 AUD (47% margin)**
- Yearly plan: $529 - $400 = **$129 AUD (24% margin)**

### Key Insights

1. **High-margin at low volume** (85-89% at 100 calls/month)
2. **Margin compression at high volume** (24-47% at 1,000 calls/month)
3. **Setup fee is pure profit** ($1,999 AUD covers onboarding, training, customization)
4. **Monthly base fee is the real revenue** (per-call fees barely cover Retell costs)

## Why Inspra's Per-Call Pricing Seems Cheap

**Inspra charges $0.18-0.25 per call, but Retell AI costs $0.55 per 5-min call.**

**How is this possible?**

### Theory 1: Volume Discounts (Most Likely)

Retell AI offers custom pricing for high-volume customers ($3,000+/month). Inspra likely has:
- Enterprise contract with Retell AI
- Discounted rate: ~$0.03-0.04 USD/min ($0.05-0.06 AUD/min)
- 5-minute call cost: $0.25-0.30 AUD (vs. $0.55 retail)

**This makes the math work:**
- Inspra charges: $0.18-0.25 per call
- Inspra pays Retell: $0.25-0.30 per call (with volume discount)
- **Inspra breaks even or slight loss on per-call** (profit comes from monthly base)

### Theory 2: Shorter Average Call Duration

If Inspra's average call is **2-3 minutes** (not 5):
- 2-minute call: $0.22 AUD (Retell cost)
- 3-minute call: $0.33 AUD (Retell cost)
- Inspra charges: $0.18-0.25 AUD
- **Inspra loses money on 3+ minute calls, profits on <2 minute calls**

### Theory 3: Monthly Base Subsidizes Per-Call

**Bundled pricing model:**
- $499/month base covers platform access, support, integrations
- Per-call fees are **below cost** to appear competitive
- Customers locked in by high setup fee ($1,999) and monthly commitment
- **Profit comes from base fee, not usage**

**Verdict:** Combination of all three - volume discounts + shorter calls + base fee subsidy

### How to Identify Retell Resellers

**Red flags:**
- ✅ Per-minute pricing similar to Retell ($0.07-0.12/min)
- ✅ Listed as Retell Certified Partner
- ✅ Mentions "powered by Retell" or "Retell AI" in tech stack
- ✅ Pricing structure: base fee + per-minute/call
- ✅ Fast time-to-market (launched in last 12 months)
- ✅ Limited customization options (standard Retell features)

**Green flags (proprietary):**
- ✅ Unique features not in Retell (e.g., Inspra's "data bank", Johnni's trade focus)
- ✅ Custom pricing (no public pricing page)
- ✅ Longer company history (3+ years)
- ✅ Own technology stack (LiveKit, custom LLM, etc.)

## Retell AI White-Label Program

### What Retell Offers

**White-Label Features:**
- Custom branding (logo, colors, domain)
- Reseller pricing (volume discounts)
- API access (full platform capabilities)
- Partner support (technical assistance)
- Co-marketing (listed on Retell partner page)

**Pricing for Resellers:**
- Not publicly disclosed
- Estimated: $0.03-0.05 USD/min ($0.05-0.08 AUD/min) for $3,000+/month volume
- Setup/partnership fee: Unknown (likely $0-5,000 USD)

### Reseller Value-Add

**What resellers add on top of Retell:**
1. **Local support** (Australian business hours, timezone)
2. **Onboarding/training** (setup, configuration, best practices)
3. **Integrations** (CRM, calendar, payment systems)
4. **Customization** (industry-specific workflows, scripts)
5. **Managed service** (ongoing optimization, monitoring)
6. **Australian data sovereignty** (compliance, local hosting)

**Typical markup:** 200-500% (e.g., Retell $0.05/min → Reseller $0.10-0.25/min)

## Build vs. Buy Analysis for Articulate AI

### Option A: Build Proprietary Platform

**Technology Stack:**
- Real-time: LiveKit
- Telephony: Telnyx
- STT: AssemblyAI or Deepgram
- LLM: GPT-4o mini or Gemini 2.0 Flash
- TTS: ElevenLabs or Cartesia

**Costs (per minute, AUD):**
- Telephony: $0.035
- STT: $0.005
- LLM: $0.008
- TTS: $0.002
- **Total: $0.050 AUD/min**

**Pros:**
- ✅ **Highest margins** (67-75% vs. 24-47% for resellers)
- ✅ **Full control** (features, pricing, roadmap)
- ✅ **Differentiation** (unique features, not commodity)
- ✅ **No vendor lock-in** (can switch providers)
- ✅ **Better unit economics** (5x lower cost than Retell)

**Cons:**
- ❌ **Higher development cost** ($50,000-100,000 initial build)
- ❌ **Longer time to market** (3-6 months vs. 2-4 weeks)
- ❌ **Technical complexity** (requires engineering team)
- ❌ **Ongoing maintenance** (updates, bug fixes, scaling)

**Recommended if:**
- Long-term vision (3+ years)
- Technical team available
- Differentiation is critical
- High-volume customers (margins matter)

---

### Option B: White-Label Retell AI

**Costs (per minute, AUD):**
- Retell AI (volume discount): $0.05-0.08 AUD/min
- **Total: $0.05-0.08 AUD/min**

**Pros:**
- ✅ **Fast to market** (2-4 weeks setup)
- ✅ **Low initial investment** ($0-5,000 setup)
- ✅ **Proven platform** (Retell is established, reliable)
- ✅ **No technical team needed** (Retell handles infrastructure)
- ✅ **Partner support** (Retell provides technical help)

**Cons:**
- ❌ **Lower margins** (24-47% vs. 67-75% proprietary)
- ❌ **Vendor lock-in** (dependent on Retell pricing, features)
- ❌ **Limited differentiation** (same platform as competitors)
- ❌ **Margin compression** (Retell can raise prices)
- ❌ **Commodity risk** (competing on price, not features)

**Recommended if:**
- Short-term goal (test market, validate demand)
- Limited technical resources
- Need revenue quickly
- Targeting low-volume customers (margins less critical)

---

### Option C: Hybrid Approach (Recommended)

**Phase 1 (Months 1-6): White-Label Retell AI**
- Launch quickly with Retell
- Validate market demand
- Build customer base
- Generate revenue ($50,000-100,000)
- Learn customer needs

**Phase 2 (Months 7-12): Build Proprietary MVP**
- Develop custom platform (LiveKit + Telnyx + AssemblyAI + GPT-4o + ElevenLabs)
- Migrate existing customers to proprietary platform
- Offer both options (Retell for low-volume, proprietary for high-volume)
- Test differentiated features

**Phase 3 (Year 2+): Full Proprietary**
- Sunset Retell partnership
- All customers on proprietary platform
- Unique features (custom LLM training, industry-specific workflows)
- Premium pricing justified by differentiation

**Pros:**
- ✅ **Best of both worlds** (fast to market + high margins long-term)
- ✅ **Lower risk** (validate before investing in proprietary)
- ✅ **Revenue funds development** (self-financing)
- ✅ **Customer continuity** (migrate existing customers)

**Cons:**
- ⚠️ **Migration complexity** (moving customers from Retell to proprietary)
- ⚠️ **Two platforms to maintain** (during transition period)
- ⚠️ **Customer confusion** (why are we changing platforms?)

## Conclusion

**Inspra.ai is a Retell AI reseller** with 85-89% margins at low volume (100 calls/month) but only 24-47% at high volume (1,000 calls/month). They profit from the $499/month base fee and $1,999 setup fee, not per-call charges.

**Recommended Strategy for Articulate AI:**

**Hybrid Approach (Best of Both Worlds):**
1. **Phase 1 (Months 1-6):** White-label Retell AI - fast to market, validate demand
2. **Phase 2 (Months 7-12):** Build proprietary platform - differentiate, increase margins
3. **Phase 3 (Year 2+):** Migrate to proprietary - full control, maximum margins

**Pricing:**
- **Starter:** $199-299 AUD/month (1,000 min included)
- **Professional:** $399-499 AUD/month (5,000 min included)
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing

**Competitive Advantages:**
- ✅ **33-88% cheaper than Inspra**
- ✅ **No setup fee** (vs. $1,999)
- ✅ **Higher margins** (37-75% vs. 24-47%)
- ✅ **Unique features** (custom LLM, industry workflows)
- ✅ **Australian data sovereignty and support**

**Expected Outcome:**
- Year 1: $100,000-200,000 revenue (Retell AI)
- Year 2: $240,000-480,000 revenue (Proprietary)
- Year 3: $500,000-1,000,000 revenue (Scale)
