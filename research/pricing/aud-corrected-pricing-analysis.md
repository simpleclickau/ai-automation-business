# AUD-Corrected Competitor Pricing Analysis

**Research Date:** November 2024  
**Exchange Rate:** 1 USD = 1.54 AUD (November 2024 average)  
**Focus:** All pricing converted to Australian Dollars for accurate comparison  
**Purpose:** Determine competitive pricing strategy for Articulate AI in Australian market

---

## Executive Summary

Most international AI voice agent platforms price in **USD**, which must be converted to **AUD** for accurate Australian market comparison. Using the current exchange rate (1 USD = 1.54 AUD), international competitors are **significantly more expensive** in AUD terms than their USD pricing suggests.

**Key Findings:**
- **Australian companies** (Inspra, Callease, Heya, etc.) already price in AUD
- **International companies** (Retell AI, Synthflow, Bland AI, Vapi) price in USD
- **Currency conversion** adds 54% to international competitor pricing
- **Articulate AI's opportunity:** Price competitively in AUD to capture Australian market

## Key Insights

### 1. Currency Conversion Impact

**International competitors are 54% more expensive in AUD:**
- Retell AI: $0.07 USD/min → **$0.11 AUD/min** (+54%)
- Synthflow: $0.08 USD/min → **$0.12 AUD/min** (+54%)
- Bland AI: $0.09 USD/min → **$0.14 AUD/min** (+54%)
- Vapi: $0.30 USD/min → **$0.46 AUD/min** (+54%)

### 2. Australian Companies Have Pricing Advantage

**No currency conversion risk:**
- Inspra.ai: Already in AUD
- Callease AI: Already in AUD
- Heya: Already in AUD
- Talk AI: Already in AUD

**Customers prefer:**
- ✅ No currency conversion fees
- ✅ No exchange rate fluctuations
- ✅ Simpler billing (same currency as their business)
- ✅ Local payment methods (BPAY, direct debit)

### 3. Inspra is Competitive at High Volumes

**For 5,000 minutes/month (1,000 calls):**
- Inspra Yearly: $529 AUD
- Retell AI: $539 AUD (comparable)
- Synthflow Growth: $984 AUD (86% more expensive)
- Bland AI Growth: $1,160 AUD (119% more expensive)

**Inspra's per-call model becomes competitive at scale** when monthly base is amortized over many calls.

### 4. Low-Volume Customers Favor Per-Minute

**For 500 minutes/month (100 calls):**
- Retell AI: $54 AUD (cheapest)
- Bland AI Developer: $70 AUD
- Synthflow Starter: $105 AUD
- Inspra Yearly: $367 AUD (579% more expensive than Retell)

**Per-minute pricing is better for low-volume customers.**
