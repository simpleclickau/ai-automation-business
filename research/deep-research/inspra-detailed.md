# Inspra.ai - Detailed Research

**Website:** https://inspra.ai/  
**Research Date:** November 5, 2025

## Company Overview

Inspra.ai provides AI-powered voice agents for business automation, focusing on sales, customer support, appointment booking, and prospecting.

## Pricing Structure (AUD, Ex-GST)

### One-Time Setup Fee
- **Cost:** $1,999 AUD per agent
- **Includes:**
  - Complete agent personalization and training setup
  - Full system integration with existing infrastructure
  - Comprehensive testing and optimization over five weeks

### Monthly Subscription
- **Cost:** $499 AUD/month
- **Includes:**
  - 25 cents/connected call (separate tier)
  - Inbound/Outbound calls
  - Local, National, and Mobile calls ONLY
  - Web-Dashboard Access
  - Core Call Recording, Transcription, and Analysis
  - Talk Bots + Chat Bots
  - Calendar Integration
  - CRM Integration
  - Premium Account Management Support
  - Expert Script Architecture, including conversational flows

### Pay-As-You-Go
- **Cost:** $0.25 AUD per connected call
- **Includes:**
  - Flat rate of twenty-five cents per call
  - Pay only for successfully connected calls made
  - No hidden charges or additional usage fees

### Enterprise Plan
- **Pricing:** Custom (contact for quote)
- **Additional Features:**
  - Dedicated Account Management Partnership
  - API Access
  - Complete Voice AI Setup & Strategic Onboarding
  - Custom Telephony - Inspra SIP Network
  - Dedicated support team

### Annual Discount
- **Save 30%** when paying annually

### Platform Features

#### Smart Dashboard
- View all calls, transcripts, and bookings in one dashboard
- See daily calls with leads converted and missed opportunities
- Track agent talk time and performance over time
- Monitor call quality and outcomes
- Voice automatically with your CRM and calendar tools

#### Call IQ
- Auto-generated summaries for every call
- Track customer sentiment and input
- Identify missed opportunities or objections
- Access full call history with transcripts
- Use call data to track agents and improve performance

#### Workflow Automation
- Send SMS or email follow-ups based on customer actions
- Automatically update CRM calendars or contact records
- Trigger actions when a lead replies, books, or misses respond
- Send reminders for next outgoing reminders or admin processes
- Fully customizable to match your operations

#### AI Chatbot
- Answers visitor questions 24/7
- Collects leads and connect live automatically
- Qualifies hopefuls and routes them to your team
- Integrates CRM to reduce friction between websites
- Helps users find exactly what they're looking for

#### Data Bank
- Create highly targeted B2B contact lists
- Filter by industry, company size, revenue, job title, and location
- Access LinkedIn profiling for precise prospecting
- Add direct phone numbers and emails for outreach
- Sync lists straight to your CRM for campaigns
