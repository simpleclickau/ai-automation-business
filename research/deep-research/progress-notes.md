- **Usage-based:** $0.07-0.25/min

- **Enterprise:** Custom (all platforms)

### Common Features
- Inbound/outbound calling
- CRM integrations
- Calendar booking
- Call analytics
- Voicemail detection
- Multi-language support
- SMS follow-up

### Differentiation Points
- **Inspra:** Australian-based, high setup fee, ROI calculator
- **Retell:** Developer-focused, API-first, $0.07/min
- **Synthflow:** No-code, visual flow builder, own telephony network, BELL framework
